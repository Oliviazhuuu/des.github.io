03. App Design.

This is a mobile-based dating app, facing people of all age who consider the dating app as their main way of meeting people. As a dating app, it includes most mainstream functions such as “look up users around you,” and “post pictures to your profile page.” However, what has really stood this app out is its ability to protect users from STDs.
