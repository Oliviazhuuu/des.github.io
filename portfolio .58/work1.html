01. Exhibition Design

This exhibition design is trying to present the everyday struggles for people who suffers from dyscalculia. As one of the major learning disabilities, dyscalculia is effecting approximately 7% of the world population. As much as this disease can brings a major downside to its patients, and there is currently no cure for it, dyscalculia is not receiving enough attention from the society as it should be. Thus, as an exhibition, the goal is to raise awareness throughout the process of experiencing.
