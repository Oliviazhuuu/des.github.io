02. Product Design

This is a product design, designated for the NFL football players. Research shows a 90% CTE rate among the NFL players, which is a serious degenerative brain disorder caused by constant concussions. With the force absorbing materials and the NS honeycomb structure; the helmet is looking to reduce at least 60% of the force from the players. In addition, with proper adjustments, the helmet can be applied to areas other than sports.
